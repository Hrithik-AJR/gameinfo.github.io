AmongUs

Among Us:Among Us is a 2018 online multiplayer social deduction game developed and published by American game studio Innersloth. Among Us takes place in a space-themed setting where players look like colorful armless cartoon astronauts.

Among Us is a multiplayer game for four to fifteen players (previously four to ten players), although recommended for at least five. Up to three players are randomly and secretly chosen to be the Impostor(s) each round.
